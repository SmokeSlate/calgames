Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about installing, running, and contributing calculator games.

What is Calculator Games?

Calculator Games is a community-powered library where you can discover, download, and learn about games for TI calculators. We highlight classic titles alongside brand new releases.

How do I download a game?

Visit the Games section, open any title, and use the download button. Each page also lists instructions for transferring the files to your calculator.

Are the games free?

Most games are completely free. If a creator charges or requests attribution, we will clearly indicate that on the game detail page.

Can I contribute a game?

Absolutely! Reach out to us through the contact form with details about your project or a link to its source. We will review and help you publish it to the catalog.
