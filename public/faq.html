Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Calculator Games?

Calculator Games is a website where you can find and download games for TI calculators. We offer a variety of games ranging from classic to new releases.

2. How do I download a game?

Simply navigate to the 'Games' section, select a game you're interested in, and follow the download link provided on the game page.

3. Are the games free?

Most of the games are free to download. However, some may have a cost associated with them, which will be clearly indicated on the game page.

4. Can I contribute a game?

Yes! If you have a game you'd like to contribute, please reach out to us through the 'Contact' page with details about your game.
